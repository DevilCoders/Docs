== Geometasearch v. 2 ==

Libraries here may be reused by old and new metasearches.

Dependencies on search-related bloatware are prohibited:
* `search/reqparam`
* `search/config`
* `search/meta` (except `search/meta/scatter`)
* `search/rank`
* etc.

Moreover, avoid the following heavy dependencies:
* `yt/yt/client`.
